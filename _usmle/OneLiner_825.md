### OneLiner

Gram-positive, optochin-sensitive, catalase-negative, coagulase-negative, Î±-hemolytic cocci growing in pairs is the characteristic appearance of Streptococcus pneumoniae, which is the most common cause of meningitis in adults, otitis media in children, bacterial pneumonia, and sinusitis.
