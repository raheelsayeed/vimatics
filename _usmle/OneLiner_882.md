### OneLiner

The superior rectal vein drains internal hemorrhoids, which are painless. Although the inferior rectal vein is the initial venous drainage for external hemorrhoids, it ultimately drains into the IVC and not the portal system. Similarly, although the internal pudendal vein is part of the external hemorrhoid drainage system, the final pathway is the IVC and not the portal system.
