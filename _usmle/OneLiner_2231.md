### OneLiner

Î”-Aminolevulinate synthase is the rate-limiting enzyme involved in heme synthesis, and it requires pyridoxine (vitamin B6) as a cofactor. A deficiency in pyridoxine, most commonly caused by isoniazid therapy, can lead to sideroblastic anemia and a cracking/scaling of the corners of the mouth (cheilosis).
